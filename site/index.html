Questions, answered.

Do I need a finished product?

No. A live app, a website, or a clear sentence about the idea all work.

How do you actually find the people who want it?

We read public conversations and search behavior — X, Reddit, YouTube comments, niche communities — alongside search demand and competitor signals, and match them to what your product does.

How current is the data?

As fresh as it gets. We surface demand from the last few days, often within hours, so the people we find are still active and still warm — not names pulled from a year-old report.

Can I trust the leads you send?

Every person we list publicly asked for or described wanting something like your product. We include the link to the original post, so you can read it and verify it yourself.

How long until I hear back?

We onboard in batches and read each submission. Your report comes by email.

Is my idea safe?

We only use what you send to build your report. We don't share it.
